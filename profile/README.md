# 🌱 Sprout AAC

We're building a free, offline-first AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) app for children who communicate differently — kids with autism, cerebral palsy, and developmental delays. No subscription. No paywall. No catches.

The leading AAC apps cost $249 or more. Research shows only **32% of minority families** have access to an AAC device, compared to 84% of white families. Sprout exists to close that gap. Every feature is permanently free and the code is open source under GPL v3 — anyone can verify that promise and hold us to it.

The app is in active development and not yet on the App Store or Google Play. [Join the waitlist](https://sproutaac.org) to be notified on launch, or come say hello in the [OpenAAC Discord](https://discord.gg/TEH8uxh).
